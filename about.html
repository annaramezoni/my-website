About me

I'm originally from Brazil, where I graduated in French Letters at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte in 2017. Between 2014 and 2018, I worked as an English teacher, a role that helped me develop strong communication skills and a love for learning.

In 2019, I moved to New Zealand and have embraced a variety of roles since then. My longest experience was as a kiwifruit orchard supervisor. Currently, I work as a fruit monitoring technician, and I'm also diving into a new chapter—studying programming and exploring new career opportunities in tech.

Outside of work and study, I’m passionate about music, staying active at the gym, and enjoying nature—especially going to the beach and snorkeling during vacations. In my free time, I also love relaxing with a good anime series.

I'm always curious and open to learning new things, whether it’s a new skill, language, or experience!
